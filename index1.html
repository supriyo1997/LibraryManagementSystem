Aboutus

The online attendance management system is based on inserting,deleting,updating and quering of a database management system.Emphasis is mainly on a real time application taking and counting of a students in class room within a short time.The main task that our project support is online attendance using bar-code authentication system .Activity scheduling is used by the administrator and lecturer for scheduling the certain task.Project monitoring includes the administratr to access all the permissions of a student and faculty.Finally by using this project, administrator can monitor and generate attendance report frequently..

Existing system

Attendance of a sudent in a classroom or seminar room is important and can be considered as the starting point towards attaining a good education.This process therefore requires attention of the instructor.Among the classical methods of taking attendance are reading out the students names and check if they are reading out the students names and checki they are present in the class .The pros and cons of the classical method is the useful interaction between students and the instructor through which the instructor can get to know the students in a same class and on the other hands, loss of time spent while taking the attendance of a crowded classes. Technolgical improvments can be useful tools to help in the developemnt of new methds to eliminate the disadvantage of the classical methods while enhancing its advantage.It is important to identify the correct tools to use in commercial and scientific studies..
